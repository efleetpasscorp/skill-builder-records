## Master hypothesis
Records that are contemporaneous, attributable, witnessed and tamper-resistant are what convert *claimed* work into *legally defensible evidence* — for inventorship/priority, regulatory audit, litigation, and R&D-tax substantiation.

## Progression of the inquiry
1. **RUN-001 (29 May)** — Reduction-to-practice & corroboration still decide priority post-AIA; editable tools are not evidentiary systems. *Confirmed.*
2. **RUN-008 (5 Jun)** — A defensible entry can be specified as an ALCOA+-derived checklist; defects are detectable against it. *Confirmed.*

## Evaluation
Master hypothesis **supported** so far: the legal doctrine (RUN-001) and the practical anatomy of a defensible entry (RUN-008) are mutually reinforcing. The study is young (2 sessions).
